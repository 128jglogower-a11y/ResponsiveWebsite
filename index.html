Top NBA MVP Candidates 2025-2026

Shai Gilgeous-Alexander

Shai Gilgeous-Alexander is regarded as one of the best players in the NBA today. He won the MVP award for the 2024-2025 season while leading his team, the OKC Thunder, to a championship over the Indiana Pacers, where he won the finals MVP. Shai is currently averaging over 30 points per game, and the Thunder are heavy favorites to win this year's championship.

Luka Doncic

Luka Doncic is a Slovenian professional basketball player who currently plays for the Los Angeles Lakers in the NBA. He is known for his exceptional playmaking skills, scoring ability, and leadership on the court. Luka has been a key player for the Mavericks and has shown significant improvement in his game over the years. He is considered one of the rising stars in the league and is often mentioned as a potential MVP candidate for the upcoming seasons.

Victor Wembanyama

Victor Wembanyama is a 22-year old, 7’6 center from France, playing his third year in the NBA. Victor is known for his wide range skillset, showing skills that centers don’t normally have, such as elite 3 point shooting and handles. It’s rare for a player of his size to be such a complete player, which is what makes him so special. He is nearly unstoppable on offense, and if you try to score on him, he’ll send your shot flying across the arena. There’s not many who can do what he does at his level.
